Our Story

A Tradition of Craft, Flavor & Gathering

What began as a small neighborhood kitchen in 2006 has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations. Chef Isabella Moreau founded {{SITE_NAME}} with a simple belief: extraordinary meals start with extraordinary ingredients and honest preparation.

Today, every plate that leaves our kitchen reflects nearly two decades of refining recipes passed down through generations, blended with bold seasonal creativity. We source from local farms within a fifty-mile radius, partner with artisan producers, and change our menu with the seasons — because the best food never stays still.

— Isabella Moreau, Founder & Executive Chef
