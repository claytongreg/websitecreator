About

Designing with intention since 2015

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and photographer based in Brooklyn. For the past eight years, I've worked at the intersection of visual identity, digital product, and image-making — helping brands find a voice that feels honest and distinctive.

My approach is rooted in restraint. I believe the best design disappears into the experience. Every project starts with listening — understanding what makes a brand singular, then stripping away everything that doesn't serve that truth.

When I'm not behind a screen or a camera, you'll find me at flea markets hunting for vintage typography specimens, running along the East River, or experimenting with film photography in my home darkroom.
